The Executive Board 2017-2018 will bring a new spin to traditional dances! If you are interested in joining the Eboard during the spring semester, feel free to contact any of the current members.

  • Paulina Lee
    President

    My name is Paulina Lee and I'm currently a junior! I’ve been a part of RCDT since my freshman year and I’ve loved every second of it! I joined RCDT, because I wanted to keep my streak of dancing through college from six years old and I also wanted to be able to meet others who had the same interests as I do. RCDT has truly become my second family and home at Rutgers, and I could not ask for anything more from my experiences with RCDT!

  • Tay Wee Siang
    Vice President/Secretary

    Hello! I am Wee Siang, a junior and the current vice president of RCDT. I enjoy performing Chinese martial arts and browsing the web for dank memes. Pleased to meet you!

  • Vivian Fujioka
    Treasurer/Artistic Director

    I'm a sophomore, double majoring in Political Science and Labor Studies. My interests involve art, nerding out over miscellaneous topics, and supporting artistic expression. RCDT is an incredible group filled with amazingly talented people, so please drop by!

  • Ngan Trieu
    Performance Coordinator

    Hi! My name is Ngan Trieu and I'm currently a junior majoring in pharmacy. My friend invited me to join RCDT and I've stuck with it ever since freshman year. I had little experience in dancing and was excited to do something out of my comfort zone. I got to meet such amazing people and spend quality time with them. They're really goofy and talented! Please check out our club when you have the time!! P.S. We also love meeting up for bubble tea so...please join:)

  • Nicholas Lee
    Public Relations Representative

    I'm a junior, double majoring in East Asian Studies (Concentrated in Chinese) and Theater in the school of Arts and Sciences. I joined RCDT my first year here because I wanted to get a new experience and to learn something new. I found RCDT at the AACC New Student Social in 2015 and I am so happy that I found RCDT. Last year I took a break from RCDT but I'm so happy to be back this year. I really hope that you all will join RCDT, no matter your ethnicity or your experience, we take everyone!

  • Nicholas Lum
    Webmaster

    I am a senior in the School of Arts and Sciences majoring in Computer Science. I was introduced to RCDT by a childhood friend who was a member of the troupe. Despite having no previous experience dancing, I quickly picked up Chinese dance with lots of help from the experienced dancers. It is now my 4th year with RCDT and I am thankful to have been in RCDT these past years. RCDT is a welcoming family and we hope to share that with you!
